# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discriminatory behavior
- Trolling or personal attacks

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via GitHub’s Security Advisory or private contact if provided.
